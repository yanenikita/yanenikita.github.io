How to join us

Advantages to be with us:

  • non-competitive participation in projects of our organization
  • lots of new useful skills and knowledge
  • exciting experience, new meetings and friends

If you want to join us, you need to:

  • share our goals and principles
  • know a foreign language (if you want to participate in a foreign project)
  • be active and initiative in the life of the organization

To join us you need to send your request (which will be reviewed within 24 hours) and then to pay the membership fee.

About us

We are the YOUTH OF BELARUS non-governmental organization.

Our mission is to render assistance to young people from any part of Belarus in implementing their ideas and initiatives based on the principles of the volunteer work, active participation and intercultural learning.

Our main activities comprise the following:

  • placement of unemployed students on the territory of the CIS countries and abroad
  • volunteer work of the young people in the region and in other countries (volunteer movement)
  • humanitarian health care projects for students, schoolchildren and orphans
  • international cooperation of young people

Every year more than 1 000 people participate in different projects of our organization.

Our partners from CIS countries as well as from other states consider our organization as a trusted and reliable partner.

We are also constantly seeking new partners for further cooperation. We are ready to consider any proposals for cooperation not only with our constant partners but also with organizations from other countries who have the same mission and activities.
